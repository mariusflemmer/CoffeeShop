Welcome

A Cup O' Baltimore has been successfully owned and operated since 2019 and welcomes you to drop by and join the locals in our fun and friendly atmosphere.

Our goal is to offer great homemade food at a reasonable price while maintaining great quality and service. Enjoy a selection of sandwiches, hot drinks, homemade desserts and daily specials.

Come join us for breakfast, lunch or an afternoon snack!
